My primary work is writing, peeling off the scales to see what covers me. I am covered in grey cat hair now(9.2025). I was covered by cactus spines a month ago because they attacked me in Sun Mountain. I write poetry and fiction, and make books and maps. I studied architecture in Seoul and New York. I am always waiting for the text from the future. I am in Brooklyn now. Contact me: sewon.work@gmail.com
